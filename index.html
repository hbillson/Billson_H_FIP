Placing a focus on helping kids and adults with ADHD to succeed...

     Attention Deficit (Hyperactive) Disorder, more commonly known as ADHD, has been known and talked about since the early 1900s, but there still lies a heavy stigma on both children and adults who live with the condition. At InFocus, we strive to break the stereotypes and lift these individuals up by raising awareness of what ADHD is really like, beyond just "bouncing off the walls" and "can't pay attention", offering plenty of sourced, up-to-date information as well as toys and tools to help ADHD individuals succeed in every aspect of their lives.

     Whether you're looking for information for yourself, or a loved one with ADHD, or if you're just curious and passing by - take a look through our webpage and learn all about ADHD, the effects it has on our population, our tips and tricks for coping, our products, and much more.
