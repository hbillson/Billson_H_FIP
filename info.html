What is ADHD?

     Attention Defecit Hyperactive Disorder was discovered in 1902. Sir George Still, a pediatrician, described it as "an abnormal defect of moral control in children.", finding that some affected children could not control their behavior the way a typical child would. He acknowledged that they were still intelligent, however.

     The estimated number of children ever diagnosed with ADHD is 6 million. (9.4%). The highest number of children diagnosed belongs to the age group of 6-11 years old. Meanwhile, the worldwide percentage of adult ADHD is estimated to be 2.4%.
